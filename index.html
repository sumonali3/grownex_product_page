Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions about supporting Gaza's orphans, answered.

How does sponsoring a Gaza orphan work?

When you sponsor a Gaza orphan, your donation covers their essential needs such as food, milk, shelter, clothing, and education. We distribute your support directly through our local partners on the ground.

Can I choose the orphan I sponsor?

To ensure fairness and that aid reaches the most vulnerable children first, we do not facilitate choosing specific orphans. Our local teams identify and prioritize children based on urgent need.

How often will I receive updates?

You will receive an initial confirmation and regular impact reports. For full sponsorships, we provide more detailed yearly updates with photos and progress of the child you are supporting.

Is my donation Zakat-eligible?

Yes, all donations made for orphan support are 100% Zakat-eligible. Our programs are verified by Islamic scholars to ensure your religious obligations are fulfilled correctly.
